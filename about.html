The thesis deals with the subject of facial expression analysis using deep learning methods. Based on the Keras library, a software was developed and implemented to classify mimic expression into one of seven basic emotions and its percentage representation is shown on a graph. The analyzed images came from the video captured in the client's Web environment using the WebRTC standard, while the used neural structures were the CNN networks, namely VGG16 and mini-Xception were compared.

28 709 monographs from the Kaggle Challenge 2013 database were used to train the CNN image classifier. The obtained accuracy was 69,49% for the mini-Xception and 67,80% for VGG16 .
